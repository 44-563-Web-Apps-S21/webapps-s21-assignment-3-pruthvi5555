Making a cup of coffee

  1. Process of making coffee
    1. place all the required items in one place
    2. Place filter in the black filter holder displayed.
    3. Place the coffee grounds in the filter.
    4. You will need to place 4 tablespoons of coffee grounds for 8 cups.
    5. Make sure the coffee grounds are evenly spread in the filter.
  2. Add water to your coffee pot.
  3. Fill the water to line 8 for 8 cups
  4. pour the water into the coffee pot carefully.
  5. sure to get all the water into the coffee maker.
  6. Place coffee pot back on burner with handle turned.
  7. Turn the coffee pot on to check the settings
    1. Choose whether you want your coffee strength strong or regular
    2. Make sure you tap the select/off button once to brew now; twice to delay brew.
    3. We will brew now.
  8. Tap the select/off button once to turn on your coffee pot.
  9. Make sure the right strength is on
  10. Make sure the brew now light is on
  • Ingridents for making coffee
    • Coffee grounds
    • Coffee filters
    • Sugar
    • Creamer
    • Coffee pot
    • Cleaning supplies
